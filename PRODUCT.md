# Product context

## Register

**brand** (marketing portfolio surface): the design is the product. Single-product showcase for **Kogo** under the **Xiaowu Dev** label: black-and-gold, editorial identity for Japanese learners and a calm first impression.

## Users

- People looking for structured Japanese study, JLPT-oriented practice, and daily rhythm on mobile.
- Developers or curious visitors checking what the author ships in spare time.

## Product purpose

Single-origin portfolio on GitHub Pages: one featured work (**Kogo**), a detail page with hero, features aligned to the real app (learn, review, practice, mocks), and store CTAs without clutter.

## Brand and tone

- Professional, restrained; cultural depth via typography, not ornament.
- No hype copy; short labels and one strong line per screen.
- Anti-reference: neon crypto vibes, generic purple-gradient SaaS heroes, dense dashboards as marketing.

## Strategic principles

- Black–gold palette with gold used sparingly for hierarchy and borders.
- Generous whitespace, readable line length, motion that supports scroll (fade/slide), never layout-thrash.
- Static-first Vite build; router + `base` documented for GitHub Pages project sites.
